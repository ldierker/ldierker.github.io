LAUREN M DIERKER
Computer Science Major · Tufts University · lauren.dierker@tufts.edu

Biography

Lauren Dierker was born in Cleveland, Ohio and graduated from Hathaway Brown School in 2014. At Hathaway Brown, Lauren was the captain and lead programmer of the FIRST Robotics team and the president of the Computer Science club. She was also involved in the Science Research and Engineering Program (SREP) through which she won the Intel Computer Science Award in 2013. Lauren is currently enrolled in Tufts University's School of Engineering as a Computer Science major and a Studio Art minor. She is a member of the Computer Science Exchange and Women in Computer Science.
